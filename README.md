# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 29-03-26

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how MyAngle (“the App”) handles information when you use it. The App is designed to allow users to measure angles on images and track progress over time.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how your data is handled.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

MyAngle does not collect or transmit personal data to external servers.

However, the App may process the following data locally on your device:

* **Images and Videos:** Photos you take or upload, and videos you select for frame extraction
* **Extracted Frames:** Images generated from selected video frames
* **Measurement Data:** Angle measurements, points, and session details you create
* **Notes and Labels:** Any text you add to sessions (e.g., movement names or notes)

All of this data is stored **only on your device** and is not sent to us or any third party.

---

## 3. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used solely to provide the core functionality of the App, including:

* Measuring angles on images
* Displaying and editing measurement overlays
* Saving and organizing sessions
* Tracking progress over time

We do not use your data for analytics, advertising, or profiling.

---

## 4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using the device’s internal storage.

We do not operate servers that store your personal data.
We do not upload, back up, or share your data externally.

You are responsible for managing and backing up your data if needed.

---

## 5. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

* **Camera:** To capture photos directly within the App
* **Photos/Media/Files:** To select images or videos from your device and to save generated images

These permissions are used strictly for the functionality described above and are never used to access data without your interaction.

---

## 6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

Your data remains entirely on your device unless you choose to share it externally using your device’s built-in sharing features.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data remains on the device and is not transmitted, we do not have access to any user data.

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the App’s features, legal requirements, or other operational reasons.

Any updates will be posted at this URL, and the “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
